About Me

Welcome to Qixuan’s homepage! I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Washington, working in Prof. Mo Li’s lab. My research focuses on integrated acousto-optic beam steering with applications in LiDAR, atomic quantum computing, and free-space optical communication.

Research Interests

  • Integrated photonics
  • Integrated Acousto-optics and Optomechanics
  • Thin-film lithium niobate (TFLN) devices
